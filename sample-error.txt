ALL POSSIBLE ERRORS:

1. Identifier too long.

2. Invalid identifier.

3. Number too long.

4. Period expected.

5. const, var, and procedure must be followed by an identifier.

6. Identifier has already been declared.

7. Constant declaration must be followed by '='.

8. '=' must be followed by a number.

9. ';' expected.

10. ':=' expected.

11. Call must be followed by identifier.

12. begin must be closed with end.

13. if condition must be followed by then.

14. while condition must be followed by do.

15. read statement must be followed by identifier.

16. relational operator expected.

17. right parenthesis missing.

18. the preceding factor cannot begin with this symbol
